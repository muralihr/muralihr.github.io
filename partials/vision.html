Plog Run

The Garden City will host its first Plog Run on October 2, on the occasion of Gandhi Jayanthi. Around 5,500 people are expected to participate in the Bengaluru Plog Run to be held in 50 locations across the city. The first of its kind, plogging combines the traditional marathon with the need to pick up plastic waste thrown in public spaces.
